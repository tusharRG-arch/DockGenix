What We Do

Comprehensive Scientific Services

Seven integrated capability areas spanning computational analysis, wet lab research, pharmaceutical quality, product development, intellectual property, training, and scientific supply.

01

Bioinformatics & Computational Biology

Molecular docking, molecular dynamics simulations, drug discovery and design, genomics, proteomics, and cheminformatics — powered by AI and Machine Learning for data analysis, predictive modeling, and optimization.

What you get

  • Ranked binding hypotheses with interaction rationale
  • MD-based stability and flexibility profiles
  • Genomic and proteomic analysis reports
  • AI/ML predictive models and screening pipelines
02

Wet Lab R&D

Microbiology, biochemistry, and molecular biology experiments including enzyme studies, fermentation processes, testing of bioactive compounds, and tissue and microbial culture techniques.

What you get

  • Assay design and experimental results
  • Enzyme characterization and activity data
  • Fermentation and bioprocess datasets
  • Documented culture and validation protocols
03

Pharmaceutical & Cosmetic Analysis

Qualitative and quantitative analysis using HPLC, GC, UV-Vis, and FTIR. Stability studies, QC/QA, and analysis of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and formulations.

What you get

  • Analytical reports (HPLC, GC, UV-Vis, FTIR)
  • Stability study data and shelf-life indicators
  • QC/QA certificates and method documentation
  • API and formulation characterization
04

Product R&D & Standardization

Research, development, validation, and standardization of pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and Ayurvedic products with industry-grade quality benchmarks.

What you get

  • Formulation development dossiers
  • Standardization and validation protocols
  • Quality benchmarks and specifications
05

Patent & IPR Consultancy

Guidance for patent applications, patent search and prior art analysis, technical support for drafting and filing, and patentability assessment for life science innovations.

What you get

  • Patent and prior-art search reports
  • Patentability assessments and opinions
  • Technical support for drafting and filing
06

Training & Skill Development

Workshops, internships, and programs covering biotechnology, bioinformatics, lab techniques, communication skills, personality development, and interview preparation.

What you get

  • Structured workshops and internship programs
  • Hands-on lab technique and bioinformatics training
  • Communication, personality, and interview preparation
07

Research Materials & Scientific Software

Manufacturing, supply, and distribution of research materials, laboratory chemicals, instruments, and scientific software for research institutions.

What you get

  • Laboratory chemicals and research materials
  • Instruments and scientific software sourcing
  • Supply and distribution support
