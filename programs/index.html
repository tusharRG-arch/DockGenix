Engagement FAQ

How do I choose the right program?

Start with your decision. If you need to know whether a hypothesis is worth pursuing, a Feasibility Sprint is usually right. If you already have priority leads and need ranking confidence, choose the Lead Optimization Program. For enzyme or bioprocess work, the Enzyme Engineering Loop is purpose-built. For anything spanning multiple institutions or aiming at publication, Collaborative Research is the fit. When in doubt, contact us and we'll recommend a path.

Can programs be customized or combined?

Yes. The models are starting points, not rigid packages. Scope, deliverables, and timelines can be tailored, and a Feasibility Sprint often flows into a Lead Optimization Program once a hypothesis is validated.

Do engagements include wet lab validation?

They can. Our defining strength is bridging in silico work with wet lab R&D — computational shortlists can flow directly into experimental validation where the program calls for it.

How is confidentiality handled?

Every engagement is confidentiality-first, with controlled data sharing, defined access points, and disciplined project communication. We support NDA-based work for sensitive programs.

Do you work with international collaborators?

Yes. Our service area is worldwide, and Collaborative Research is designed for national and international, multi-institutional projects requiring India-based computational and experimental support.
