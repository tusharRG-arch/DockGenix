Questions

Before an engagement begins

Are timelines fixed?

No. Published ranges are planning references. A proposal should confirm timing after data, scope, methods, dependencies, and review cycles are understood.

Can experimental work be included?

Potentially. Applicable methods, facilities, responsibilities, sample requirements, and access must be confirmed for the project.

Can work begin under an NDA?

NDA-based discussions can be considered. Do not submit sensitive information until the appropriate agreement and transfer route are in place.
