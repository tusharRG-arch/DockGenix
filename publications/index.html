Molecular Docking & Dynamics of Breynia patens

Field: Computational Pharmacology Source: ScienceDirect

This study applies molecular docking and molecular dynamics simulations to investigate a potential galactagogue mechanism of Breynia patens, examining modulation of the dopamine D2 receptor (D2R), oxytocin receptor (OXTR), and thyroid hormone receptor beta (TRbeta).
