Privacy notice

How website interactions may be handled

This interim notice describes the public website as currently implemented. It is not a claim of compliance with any particular legal regime.

Last reviewed15 July 2026Contact dataTechnical dataExternal servicesYour choices

Contact data

The current website does not provide an active online intake form. If you contact DockGenix by email, the information is processed through the applicable email providers and retained as needed to respond, assess the enquiry, and maintain appropriate business records.

Do not send confidential structures, unpublished datasets, sensitive research information, or proprietary attachments before an NDA and suitable transfer route are in place.

Technical request data

The website and its hosting or security providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser information, requested pages, timestamps, and security signals to deliver and protect the site.

External services

The production domain is served through Cloudflare, which may provide content delivery, security, email-address protection, and web analytics. WhatsApp links open a separate service governed by its own terms and privacy practices.

The repository does not currently set first-party marketing cookies or submit contact-form data to Web3Forms.

Your choices and questions

You can avoid WhatsApp and contact DockGenix by email. For questions about website data handling, contact info@dockgenix.in.

Review status

Registered entity information, formal retention periods, privacy-contact designation, and jurisdiction-specific disclosures require company and professional review before this notice is considered complete.
