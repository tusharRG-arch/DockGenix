Capability 05

Analytical & product R&D

Project-specific characterization and development support with agreed methods, quality criteria, access, and reporting boundaries.

Problems addressed

Connect an analytical question to a defensible method and report

Instrument names alone do not establish suitability, validation, or accreditation. Each scope should define the method status and the decision the result supports.

Suitable stages

Early characterization, method exploration, formulation or product-development support, and comparative quality questions.

Typical audiences

Pharmaceutical, cosmetic, Ayurveda, biotechnology, and academic product-research teams.

Methods that may be evaluated

HPLCGCUV-VisFTIRStability planningProduct characterization

Quality controls to define

  • Method status and suitability
  • Reference materials and acceptance criteria
  • Instrument/facility access and responsibility
  • Report type and permitted interpretation

Quality and regulatory boundary

No accredited certificate, regulatory acceptance, validated method, or formal shelf-life claim is implied unless explicitly supported and agreed for the project.

Inputs to discuss
  • Product or sample matrix
  • Analyte or quality question
  • Applicable references
  • Method history
  • Required report and use
