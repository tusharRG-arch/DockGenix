Intended workflow

From question to reviewable hypothesis

  1. 01

    Frame

    Ask a bounded question with organism, pathway, and objective context.

  2. 02

    Retrieve

    Identify relevant concepts and supporting knowledge sources.

  3. 03

    Synthesize

    Present a structured answer with alternatives and uncertainty.

  4. 04

    Review

    Check sources and decide what expert analysis or experiment is needed.

Research-use boundary

MetaboAgent is intended for research exploration. It is not clinical, diagnostic, regulatory, or autonomous scientific authority. Any output must be checked against relevant primary sources and expert judgment.
