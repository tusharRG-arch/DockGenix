Our Philosophy

In Silico to Wet Lab

Computational predictions are powerful, but predictions alone do not de-risk a research program. DockGenix exists to close the loop — pairing in silico intelligence such as molecular docking, dynamics simulations, and AI/ML modeling with wet lab validation in microbiology, biochemistry, and analytical chemistry.

This integrated model means a hypothesis generated at the computer is stress-tested at the bench, and experimental findings feed back into refined models. Teams get a single scientific partner across the full discovery cycle instead of stitching results together from disconnected vendors.

We work with pharmaceutical, biotech, academic, industrial, cosmetic, and Ayurvedic organizations — supporting research, development, quality analysis, patent consultancy, and skill development for both national and international collaborators.

Founded in 2026 with a worldwide service area, DockGenix is built from the ground up around reproducibility, confidentiality, and decision-grade documentation.
