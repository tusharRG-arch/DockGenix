Modern biological research demands a molecular-resolution understanding of life. Drug Discovery & Biopharmaceutical R&D

Molecular Docking

Molecular docking predicts the preferred binding orientation of small molecules, peptides, or proteins within a biological target. It enables hypothesis-driven screening of chemical space prior to experimental validation, reducing cost and time.

Molecular Dynamics Simulation (MDS)

MDS captures atomic-level motions of biomolecules over time, revealing conformational flexibility, stability, and binding persistence beyond static structures. Essential for understanding induced-fit mechanisms and allosteric regulation.

Virtual Screening & Binding Free Energy

High-throughput virtual screening combined with MM-PBSA / MM-GBSA calculations enables quantitative ranking of ligands based on thermodynamic binding affinity, improving lead prioritization accuracy.

SAR, QSAR & Pharmacophore Modeling

Structure–activity relationships reveal how chemical modifications influence biological response, while QSAR and pharmacophore models abstract these principles into predictive frameworks guiding rational lead optimization.

ADMET & Drug-Likeness

Early assessment of absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion, and toxicity reduces late-stage clinical failures by filtering compounds with unfavorable pharmacokinetic or safety profiles.

Industrial Biotechnology & Enzyme Engineering

Enzyme–Substrate Docking & Mutagenesis

Computational enzyme engineering enables rational design of catalytic efficiency, substrate specificity, and thermostability by evaluating residue-level interactions prior to wet-lab mutagenesis.

Metabolic Pathway & Flux Analysis

Systems-level pathway modeling and flux balance analysis reveal metabolic bottlenecks and optimization strategies for industrial bioprocesses.

Agro-Biotechnology & Microbial Systems

Host–Pathogen & Network Pharmacology

Network-based approaches uncover multi-target intervention strategies in plant and microbial systems, supporting sustainable disease management.

Antimicrobial Peptides & Resistance Analysis

Computational modeling of antimicrobial peptides and resistance genes aids in the design of next-generation bioactive molecules against evolving pathogens.

Bioinformatics, Genomics & Systems Biology

From genome annotation to comparative genomics and transcriptomics, DockGenix deciphers biological information encoded across molecular hierarchies using statistically rigorous and biologically interpretable workflows.

Synthetic Biology & Molecular Design

In silico design of primers, vectors, codon-optimized genes, and CRISPR elements accelerates experimental design while minimizing trial-and-error.
