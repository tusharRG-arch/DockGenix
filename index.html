MetaboAgent: Evidence-Grounded Biochemistry Research

A public DockGenix research agent built on the Gemma 4 family for pathway reasoning, compound lookup, enzyme analysis, and literature-grounded biochemical research.

Gemma 4 Good Hackathon Submission

Published in May 2026 at Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai, as a reproducible research system and scientific evaluation platform.

Live Product

MetaboAgent

An evidence-grounded biochemistry research agent built on the Gemma 4 family. Ask about metabolic pathways, compounds, enzymes, or current literature with citations linked back to source databases.

Try It Live View Source Code
  • PubMed grounding with 54,000 indexed abstracts plus live NCBI E-utilities.
  • KEGG-backed retrieval across compounds, reactions, and pathways.
  • UniProt protein evidence for enzyme and function-level queries.
  • Streaming answers with citations populated in real time.
Architecture

4x NVIDIA L40 Deployment

MetaboAgent runs on a multi-tier Gemma 4 stack tuned for routing, tool use, multimodal inputs, and multi-step biochemical reasoning.

  • Gemma 4 E4B for routing, function calling, and multimodal inputs
  • Gemma 4 26B MoE for deeper reasoning and pathway synthesis
  • 15 tools spanning PubMed, KEGG, UniProt, BLAST, and identifier checks
Research Findings

Built as a Measurement Platform

The system was used to publish findings about scientific grounding quality in LLM workflows, not just to ship a demo interface.

  • Vision LLMs are not reliable OCSR systems under strict structure matching.
  • Existence-only citation checks can hide severe semantic hallucination.
  • Every numerical claim traces to JSON artifacts under the evaluation pipeline.
Reproducibility

Open Evaluation Workflow

The project is Apache 2.0 licensed with a one-command evaluation path for reproducing the reported benchmark outputs.

python -m eval all --report

Approximate runtime is 12 minutes on one L40-class GPU, with outputs stored under eval/results/.
